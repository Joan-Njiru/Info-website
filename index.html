About Us
Mboga On Wheels

Mboga On Wheels is an online grocery store that allows customers to get access to mama mboga’s products and services at the convenience of their own home. In Kenya, 62.6% of the working population work full time jobs and this means that they barely get time to physically shop for their own groceries. Mboga On Wheels has addressed this issue by offering a solution that meets the requirements of the entire population.In addition to having a physical shop at Ruaka,Kiambu, you can access all our products and services at —-link for mboga on wheels– where you get to select all the goods that you need and get them delivered to you within no time.Our goal is to provide our customers with a wide variety of high quality products that are accessible to our customers all over the country.

We Value Loyalty!

Buy On Credit

At Mboga On Wheels, we place very high value on loyalty. We therefore find different ways of rewarding our customers which includes providing them with credit services when they're shopping. This means that they can buy products even if they don't have enough money as long as it's within their credit limit at the time. with time, we give our customers the chance to grow their credit limit so that they have the ability of buying more products on credit.

Fast and Efficient Delivery Services

We offer fast, efficient, same-day delivery services to all our customers. Our products come in clean and secure packaging to ensure the safety of our customers at all times. Our delivery team is composed of amazing people who are willing to keep you updated on your packages at any stage of the delivery process to ensure they get to you on time. At Mboga on wheels, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Lastly,we offer fair delivery prices depending on our customers' location.
