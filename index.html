About Us
Mboga On Wheels

Mboga On Wheels is an online grocery store that allows customers to get access to mama mboga’s products and services at the convenience of their own home. In Kenya, 62.6% of the working population work full time jobs and this means that they barely get time to physically shop for their own groceries. Mboga On Wheels has addressed this issue by offering a solution that meets the requirements of the entire population.In addition to having a physical shop at Ruaka,Kiambu, you can access all our products and services at —-link for mboga on wheels– where you get to select all the goods that you need and get them delivered to you within no time.Our goal is to provide our customers with a wide variety of high quality products that are accessible to our customers all over the country.
